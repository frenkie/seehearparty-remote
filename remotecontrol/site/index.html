See Hear Party As A Service

If you haven't checked out See Hear Party yet, go do it! It combines GIF's from Giphy and music from SoundCloud into an energetic, epileptic and epic party visualizer. Made with <3 by @peterjavidpour;

Remote control

One thing missing is a remote, so you and your friends can fight over which GIF to see or sound to hear.

Well now it's here! With the See-Hear-Party-Remote you can manage the GIF tag input, the SoundCloud search query, or navigate to the next track from multiple devices.

Usage

The See Hear Party site only works in the Chrome browser for now, so step 1 and 2 should be done in Chrome.

1

Save the following bookmarklet:

See Hear Party

2

Visit See Hear Party; start the party; press the saved Bookmark and name your party place.

3

Visit the remote control with other devices, enter your party name, and start fighting over the remote!



P.S.

You can even connect multiple See Hear Party sites with the bookmarklet, and share parties in each other's house!
